The takeaway

At every constrained optimum, the gradients are parallel:

\[ \nabla f(x^\ast, y^\ast) \;=\; \lambda \, \nabla g(x^\ast, y^\ast) \]

And the multiplier \(\lambda\) has a meaning:

\[ \lambda \;=\; \frac{d f^\ast}{d c} \]

("shadow price" — how much the optimum value changes per unit of the resource c)
